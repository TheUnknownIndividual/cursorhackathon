Azerbaijan's Active Consumer Support Mechanism

Good news! Azerbaijan implemented a net-metering system allowing you to sell excess solar energy to Azərenerji and use it later when needed.

Key Benefits:

  • System size: Install solar panels up to ~150 kW for residential/commercial use
  • Surplus energy: Any electricity you generate but don't use is fed into the national grid
  • Night usage: The surplus offsets your consumption later—essentially "banking" energy for nighttime use
  • Financial savings: Reduce or eliminate your electricity bills through net-metering

Legal framework: Approved by Cabinet of Ministers Decree No. 346 (September 28, 2023).
